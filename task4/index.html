School Details


Vidyadham Prashala, Shirur

Vidyadham Prashala is a reputed secondary school located in Shirur, Pune, Maharashtra. The school is affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) and offers classes from Standard 5 to 10. It focuses on providing quality education, discipline, and extracurricular activities to ensure all-round development of students. The institution has well-structured classrooms, science laboratories, a library, and sports facilities. Teachers emphasize both academic excellence and moral values, preparing students for board examinations and future studies. Vidyadham Prashala has consistently achieved good results in SSC examinations.

Trimurti Junior College of Science and Arts

Trimurti Junior College of Science and Arts, located in Shirur, Pune (Maharashtra), is a well-established institution offering Higher Secondary Education (11th and 12th) under the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE). The college provides streams in Science, Arts, and Commerce, focusing on strong academic foundations as well as extracurricular development.

With experienced faculty, laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, along with a well-equipped library and computer facilities, the college ensures students are well-prepared for competitive exams such as JEE, NEET, and CET. The institution emphasizes discipline, career guidance, and holistic education, helping students transition smoothly into higher education and professional careers.

College Details


Chandmal Tarachand Bora College, Shirur

Chandmal Tarachand Bora College (CT Bora College), established in 1968, is a reputed institution located in Shirur, Pune, Maharashtra. It is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Science, Arts, and Commerce.

The college is known for its dedicated faculty, strong academic culture, well-equipped laboratories, computer facilities, and a rich library. It provides excellent opportunities for students to participate in NSS, NCC, cultural programs, and research activities. The institution emphasizes not only academics but also the holistic development of students through sports, seminars, and career guidance.
